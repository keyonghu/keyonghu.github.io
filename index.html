Hi, there! I am currently an Associate Professor with School of Information and Control Engineering at Qingdao University of Technology, Qingdao, China. I received the B.E. degree in computer science and technology from Ocean University of China in 2008, the M.S. and Ph.D, degree in computer application technology from Ocean University of China in 2010 and 2014, respectively.

Research

My research interests lie in AI for Internet of Things (IOT), Urban Computing and Signal Processing . I am currently working on the following topics.
  • Imputation and prediction for time series data
  • Modelling and classification for industrial audio data
  • AI applications for large language models
