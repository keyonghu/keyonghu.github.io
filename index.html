Hi, there! I am currently an Associate Professor with School of Information and Control Engineering at Qingdao University of Technology, Qingdao, China. I received the B.E. degree in computer science and technology from Ocean University of China in 2008, the M.S. and Ph.D, degree in computer application technology from Ocean University of China in 2010 and 2014, respectively. My research interests include urban computing, underwater networks and statistical inference.
