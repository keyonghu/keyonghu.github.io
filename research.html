Research interests

My research interests lie in the general area of Internet of Things (IOT), Urban Computing and Machine Learning. I am currently working on the following urban computing topics.
  • Air quality reconstruction and prediction
  • UAV based urban intelligent management
  • General-purpose urban knowledge distillation

Research grants

  • Research and development of basic machine learning library based on C++ under Kylin operating system, Changchun Institute of Optics, Fine Mechanics and Physics. Principle Investigator.

  • Research on key technology for spatial-temporal joint reconstruction of air quality, National Nature Science Foundation of China, 2020.01 - 2022.12, Principle Investigator.

  • Research on land use change simulation model considering the spatial differentiation of urban function in the study area, Shandong Provincial Natural Science Foundation, 2019.07 - 2022.06, Principle Participator.

  • Research on key technology of Energy-efficient and accurate localization for mobile underwater acoustic sensor networks, Shandong Provincial Natural Science Foundation, 2016.11 - 2018.11, Principle Investigator.
