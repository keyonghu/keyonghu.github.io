Research interests

My research interests lie in the general area of Internet of Things (IOT) and Machine Learning. I am currently working on urban computing, underwater networks and statistical inference.
  • Air quality reconstruction and prediction
  • Underwater network localization and navigation
  • Gaussian and non-parametric belief propagation
  • Bayesian matrix and tensor factorization
